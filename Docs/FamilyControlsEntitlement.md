# Family Controls 权限申请话术

以下文字可供开发者在 Apple Developer 权限申请或 App Review 备注中使用。

## 用途说明

This app is a parental control tool that helps parents or guardians manage access to selected apps, websites, and activity categories on a child device. The parent or guardian explicitly selects what to manage using Apple's FamilyActivityPicker. The app applies restrictions through Apple's Family Controls and Managed Settings frameworks.

## 隐私说明

The app does not collect, upload, sell, or share a child's app usage history, browsing content, messages, screenshots, screen recordings, location, or personal communications. Selected apps and websites are represented by Apple's privacy-preserving tokens and are stored locally on the device.

## 控制说明

The app does not attempt to bypass Screen Time privacy protections. It does not inspect traffic content and does not use private APIs. Restrictions are applied only after the required Family Controls authorization flow.

## 审核备注

The first version only supports selecting and shielding apps, websites, and categories. It does not claim to throttle network speed on iOS.
